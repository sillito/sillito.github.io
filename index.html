We are a company focused on intense face-to-face learning in Calgary. Our courses are for teams that want to quickly get started making software on a new platform, or just explore new ways to build software.

Developing great software starts with great people. People that deeply understand their languages, frameworks and tools. But also people that understand the best ideas from other frameworks and the field of computer science generally.

Our getting started courses help participants develop their first a application in a new language or framework. At the same time the fundamental principles of the technology will be covered for a strong foundation for continued learning. Our hands on courses include:

  • Getting started with Ruby on Rails. Three days on the basics of the Ruby programming language and the Ruby on Rails web framework. By the end of the course you'll have developed your first Rails application and learned the key patterns and conventions at the heart of the framework.

  • Getting started with iOS programming. Four intense days on the fundamentals of the Objective-C programming language along with the core tools and frameworks needed for developing iPhone and iPad applications. The course will cover the object-oriented design patterns at the heart of the iOS frameworks so you'll understand how and why things work.

  • Getting started with JavaScript and Node.js. Two intense days on the fundamentals of developing server software, including web applications, with Node.js. By the end of the course you'll deeply understand Node's nonblocking, evented execution model.
